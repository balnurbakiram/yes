Fly Yoga

Experience the freedom of movement and deep relaxation with Fly Yoga, an innovative practice that combines traditional yoga, aerial acrobatics, and Pilates. Suspended in a soft hammock, you'll flow through poses that enhance flexibility, build strength, and relieve tension—all while feeling weightless.

FLY YOGA STUDIO

What will you get?

Flexibility Fly Yoga improves joint mobility, stretches muscles, and enhances overall body coordination.
Strength Aerial poses engage deep muscles, strengthening the core and improving endurance.
Balance Hanging in the air helps refine balance, develop stability, and boost concentration.
Lightness The anti-gravity effect reduces spinal compression, improves posture, and creates a feeling of weightlessness and relaxation.
